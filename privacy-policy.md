# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-28

DriveVector respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect
DriveVector may access the following information on your device:
- Location information for GPS speed and driving telemetry display
- Motion and sensor data for acceleration, orientation, and vehicle behavior visualization
- App usage data related to logging, settings, and app functionality
- Advertising-related identifiers when ads are shown
- Purchase information for premium feature access

## How We Use Information
We use this information to:
- Display speed, G-force, attitude, friction circle, and graph data
- Save and export driving logs
- Provide premium feature access
- Show advertisements for non-premium users
- Improve app stability and functionality

## Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal information.

Some data may be processed by third-party services used by the app, including:
- Apple
- Google AdMob
- In-App Purchase services

## Data Storage
Driving logs and settings may be stored on your device.
Exported files are handled by the apps and services you choose to share them with.

## Your Choices
You can:
- Deny location permission
- Deny motion permission
- Delete exported log files
- Stop using the app at any time
