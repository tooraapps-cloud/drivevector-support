# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-28

DriveVector respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect
DriveVector may access or process the following information on your device:
- Location information for GPS speed and driving telemetry display
- Motion and sensor data for acceleration, orientation, and vehicle behavior visualization
- App settings and usage information related to logging, calibration, language, and app functionality
- Advertising-related identifiers when ads are shown to non-premium users
- Purchase information required to enable premium features

## How We Use Information
We use this information to:
- Display speed, G-force, attitude, friction circle, and graph data
- Save and export driving logs
- Provide premium feature access
- Show advertisements for non-premium users
- Improve app stability and functionality

## Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal information.

Some data may be processed by third-party services used by the app, including:
- Apple
- Apple In-App Purchase
- Google AdMob

These services may process limited technical or device-related information as required to provide their functionality.

## Data Storage
Driving logs and app settings may be stored on your device.
Exported files are handled by the apps and services you choose to share them with.

## Your Choices
You can:
- Deny location permission
- Deny motion permission
- Delete exported log files
- Stop using the app at any time
